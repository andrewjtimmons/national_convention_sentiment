Presidential Candidate Speech Sentiment

These are the results of using Google's Natrual Language Processing API to analyze the sentiment of Clinton's and Trump's Convention Speech. Google's API believes that Hillary Clinton's DNC speech was substanially more positive than Donald Trump's RNC speech and more passionate.
Polarity is the measure of how negative or positive the emotion of the speech is. It is on a scale of -1 (very negative) to 1 (very positive).
Negative
Neutral
Positive
Magnitude is the strength of the emotion. It is independant of polarity and is on a scale of 1 to infinity.
